    Co-Founder & Former Project Manager

    About

    Pramod is a very dedicated, hardworking individual who always gives his everything to the team and works towards it's improvement continuously. Blessed with natural leadership and managerial qualities, he adds a secret ingredient called friendship which makes it a fun-filled ride for the members of the team.   • Kai Maitreya Hegde

    Kai Maitreya Hegde

    Co-Founder & Former Chief Engineer

    About

    A person with an infinite thirst for knowledge, Kai Maitreya Hegde's helping nature doesn't make him think twice before sharing his knowledge to any of his peers. He personifies the fact that work becomes easier when it is enjoyed. His passion for Astrobiology is well known throughout the team. Apart from his meticulous working style, he is an avid gamer and also a big fan of rock music.
    He has also authored two research papers, both presented at international conferences. Kai, is currently pursuing his Master's in Aerospace Engineering at Delft University of Technology, The Netherlands.

    About

    A silent person with a great quality of selflessness especially towards friends. A highly optimistic person who always dons a smile on his face whatsoever.
