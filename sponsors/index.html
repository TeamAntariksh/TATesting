How Can You Help Us?

Monetary Contribution

Monetary contributions go a long way in covering our day to day expenses and logistics fee.

Expertise


As a purely Undergraduate Club, Team Antariksh welcomes any knowledge and experience offered. We pride ourselves in our ability to learn.

Contributions In Kind

We, at Team Antariksh love donations of equipments/supplies. We believe this builds the strongest and most productive relationship with our benefactors.
